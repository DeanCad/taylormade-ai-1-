## Next Steps for Maximum SEO Impact

### **Immediate (This Week)**
1. **Submit to Google Search Console**
   - Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
   - Add your domain: aitaylormade.com
   - Verify ownership (add DNS record or HTML file)
   - Submit sitemap.xml
   - Request indexing for homepage

2. **Set Up Google Business Profile**
   - Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
   - Create/claim your business listing
   - Add: business name, address, phone, hours, categories
   - Add photos and description
   - Verify your business

3. **Update Social Media Links**
   - Add your social media URLs to the footer/contact section
   - Create LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter profiles
   - Link back to your website

### **Short Term (1-2 Weeks)**
1. **Create High-Quality Content**
   - Blog posts on: "How AI Agents Save Time", "ROI of Voice Agents", etc.
   - Target long-tail keywords with low competition
   - Aim for 1,500+ words per post
   - Include internal links to pricing/contact pages

2. **Build Backlinks**
   - Reach out to Australian tech/business blogs for guest posts
   - Submit to directories: Australian Business Register, local directories
   - Get mentioned on industry websites

3. **Optimize Page Load Speed**
   - Test at [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev/)
   - Compress images further if needed
   - Enable caching

### **Medium Term (1-3 Months)**
1. **Content Marketing**
   - Publish weekly blog posts
   - Create video content (Dean/Robbie testimonials)
   - Develop case studies with client results

2. **Local SEO**
   - Get reviews on Google Business Profile
   - List on local Perth business directories
   - Create location-specific landing pages if expanding

3. **Link Building**
   - Reach out to industry influencers
   - Create shareable assets (infographics, guides)
   - Build relationships with complementary businesses

## Key SEO Metrics to Track

1. **Google Search Console**
   - Impressions (how often you appear in search results)
   - Click-through rate (CTR)
   - Average position in search results
   - Indexed pages

2. **Google Analytics**
   - Organic traffic (visitors from Google Search)
   - Conversion rate (form submissions)
   - Average session duration
   - Bounce rate

3. **Ranking Tools** (Optional)
   - Track keyword rankings over time
   - Monitor competitor rankings
   - Tools: SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz (paid), or Google Search Console (free)
